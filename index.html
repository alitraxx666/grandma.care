Grandma.care is a simple caregiver and senior tablet app designed to help older adults stay safer, connected, and supported at home.

Phase One is designed for a caregiver’s phone and a dedicated senior tablet that can stay visible and available throughout the day.

Built for real families.

Grandma.care helps families support seniors with memory loss, confusion, or low comfort with technology — without taking away independence.

Simple by design

No complicated menus, no technical setup for the senior, and no assumption that they can navigate modern devices on their own.

Caregiver connected

Trusted family members can send reminders, check-ins, and messages from the caregiver app to the senior’s tablet.

Safety focused

Designed to support daily routines, reduce confusion, and help families notice when something may need attention.

Phase One Prototype

The first version of Grandma.care uses two connected apps: a caregiver app for a family member’s phone and a senior tablet app designed to run throughout the day on an iPad, Android tablet, or older dedicated tablet.

Families can place the tablet somewhere visible in the home so reminders, check-ins, and family messages are easy for the senior to see and respond to. The goal is to make support feel calm, familiar, and simple.

Future Vision

Future versions of Grandma.care may expand beyond standard tablets into a dedicated senior-friendly device: simple, portable, always-on, and designed specifically for care reminders, safety check-ins, and family connection at home or on the go.
